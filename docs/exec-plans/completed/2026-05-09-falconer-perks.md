# Falconer Perks

## Goal

Add Falconer as the Flying-type job with rank-scaled catch/mount perks plus Feather Falling-lite, Slow Fall-lite, High Ground, and Scout's Leap.

## Scope

- Job defaults and runtime TOML.
- Role perk UI text.
- Server-side fall damage, slow falling, high-Y speed, and sprint jump boost.
- Docs.
